How do I invite Jacques to my server?

Click the invite button on the toolbar.

How do I play a sound?

An exclamation point denotes a "command" which Jacques will attempt to interpret. To play a sound, you type an exclamation point followed by the name of the sound. If your Discord server has a sound of that name, Jacques will join the voice channel, play the sound, then leave. If you want Jacques to stop, use !cancel or !stop.
  • !yeah plays the sound named "yeah" in your Guild.
  • !fif plays the sound named "fif" in your Guild.
  • !cancel forces Jacques to fly away.
Unless you enter the command in a channel called commands, Jacques will delete it shortly after.

How do I submit a sound for my server?

First, type !birdfeed in any text channel on that server. Jacques should send you a private message containing a token like "a1934u67b9". Now, click the birdfeed icon on the toolbar to feed Jacques. This is pretty much a poor man's login to make sure you're part of the guild and have at least the 'Attach Files' permission.
Now, you can visit the Upload page to submit your sound. Drag and drop the sound into the box or click the 'Browse' button to locate it. Sounds must be MP3 files and under 700KB, so make sure it isn't too long.
To delete a sound, browse for it, then click the garbage can icon.

What about YouTube Videos?

Similar to sound clips, start with an exclamation point. However, instead of the name of a sound, use the word "stream." Then, paste your YouTube video's URL. You can include the exact time to start, if desired.
  • !stream https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BJ0xBCwkg3E plays the entire Running in the 90's video.
  • !stream https://youtu.be/BJ0xBCwkg3E?t=1m1s plays the same video starting at 1 minute and 1 second.

He's too loud (or quiet) for this video!

Use the "volume" command to view Jacques' current volume level, from 0 to 100. You can then tweak the volume level by including a value after the word "volume." Keep in mind this is only for YouTube videos. Sound clips should be submitted at their desired volume level.
  • !volume causes Jacques to say his current volume level.
  • !volume 60 sets the volume to 60%.
