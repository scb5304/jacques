Want to upload a sound for your Discord server? Make sure you have fed Jacques! Click the birdfeed icon on the toolbar. Once you've given him birdfeed, you're good to go.

Each of your files' names will become their sound names. For example, if you upload "mySound.mp3", it can then be played with mySound. For additional help, visit the help page.

At least one file is too large. Cannot be more than 700KB.
